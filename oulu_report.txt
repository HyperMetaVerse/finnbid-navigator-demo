EXEC_SUMMARY
The City of Oulu published a national open-procedure tender on 2 April 2026 for civil engineering works covering street reconstruction, environmental works, and lighting across two sites valued at approximately €900,000. The contract duration is 5 months and award goes solely to the lowest total price. The submission deadline of 23 April 2026 at 12:00 has already passed, making this tender currently closed. Two unresolved regulatory issues add further risk: a zoning appeal may potentially block works on Solistinkatu's northern section, and the Rata-aukio 2 building permit is still being processed. Full suitability and qualification criteria are not disclosed in the notice and must be retrieved from Cloudia Competition. Subject to a corrigendum extending the deadline, the opportunity appears operationally feasible for an experienced civil contractor. However, the regulatory uncertainty and unknown qualification threshold require verification before any bid commitment. No EU funding is involved and SME participation is explicitly permitted.
